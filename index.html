FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does junk removal cost?
Pricing depends on the volume and type of items. We offer free estimates — chat with us or call and we'll give you a clear price before we start. No surprise charges.
Do you offer same-day service?
In many cases, yes! Our hours are Monday–Friday 5pm–8pm and Saturday–Sunday 7am–5pm. Give us a call to check availability for your area.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Southern New Hampshire and Northern Massachusetts. If you're not sure whether we cover your town, just call us and we'll let you know.
Do I need to be home during the pickup?
For most jobs, yes — someone should be on-site to show us what needs to go. We'll coordinate a time that works with your schedule.
How do I get started?
Use the chat widget above, call 603-494-6776, or email Mcgowancormierjunk@gmail.com. You can also text us photos of your items for a quick estimate.
Are there items you won't take?
We handle almost everything, but we cannot remove hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, fuel tanks, or asbestos. When in doubt, just ask us before booking.
